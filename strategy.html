Strategy

  • Intelligent Connected System in All Product Lineup

    Equipped with 4G intelligent connected system, all HAVAL product lineup have a series of functions such as intelligent voice, online navigation, QQ Music, Koala FM and intelligent remote control, which makes driving safer and more convenient, and greatly enriches the users’ pleasant driving experience.

  • HI-LIFE Intelligent Connected System

    As a complete AI Internet of Vehicles system, HI-LIFE ,the new generation of intelligent connected system, has access to Baidu's powerful ecology of content, service and application, providing users with a terrific vehicle-mounted interactive experience.

  • 1.5GDIT Engine

    The 1.5GDIT engine independently developed by Great Wall Motors won the title of China Best Ten Engine 2017. The 1.5GDIT engine owns 92 patents and has complete intellectual property rights. CVVL independently developed by Great Wall Motor is adopted. Currently, only a few foreign brands such as BMW have similar engine technology. CVVL not only reduces fuel consumption by 8.4%, but also improves maximum power by 12.7% and peak torque by 35.7%.

  • 7DCT

    The 7DCT independently developed by Great Wall Motors is awarded as the World Best Ten Transmission. It is the first dual-clutch transmission independently developed by Chinese brands in a true sense, with an integrated transmission rate up to 95% and ultra-high system reliability. The 7DCT adopts a system-level energy consumption reduction scheme, including the use of low friction bearing and oil seal, low leakage solenoid valve, low inertia dual clutch module, ultra-low viscosity lubricating oil, dual pump system, low oil volume design, achieving extremely high transmission efficiency.

  • ALL-ROUND SAFETY

    Adhering to the vehicle manufacturing concept of “safety first”, HAVAL aims to produce the safest SUV. HAVAL has established the design concept of "passenger and driver safety, pedestrian safety and vehicle safety three-dimensional protection" (3DP) and took the lead in establishing the vehicle safety technology R&D system and the safety performance positive development process in the industry. So far, Great Wall Motors has been granted with 114 patents and established 75 enterprise standards in safety. Meanwhile, HAVAL has formed a complete set of test, development and verification system from automobile safety components, system to vehicle, which not only meets the regulations and standards and NCAP test and assessment requirements of China, the European Union and the United States, but also can be used for the reproduced test of a series of actual traffic accidents.

HAVAL brand has been dedicating to build a high quality , exquisite and professional product lineup, and win the consumers' trust by building a brand service of "moving the customers".
