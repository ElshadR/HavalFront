  • R&D Investment

    Based on the strategy of excessive investment, HAVAL has established 8 R&D centers and technology incubation laboratories in many countries, covering intelligent driving, connectivity, new energy, modeling and other fields. Within the next five years, HAVAL's R&D investment in the world is expected to exceed 30 billion yuan. In the future, HAVAL will set up a new technology incubation laboratory in Silicon Valley, and conduct cutting-edge technologies research in cooperation with international technology giants.

  • HAVAL R&D Center in China

    As one of the largest and most advanced automobile comprehensive R&D base in China so far, HAVAL Research and Development Center has five functions including R&D, trial production, test, modeling and data.There are 14 types of large-scale comprehensive testing laboratories for performance, NVH&BSR, reliability & durability, new energy and energy saving

  • High Performance Proving Ground

    GWM invested 1 billion yuan in building the PG, covering an area about 1.14 million square meters, including 11 modules, such as high-speed circuit, dynamic platform, test hills track, city square and handling track, with more than 100 typical test pavements and a total test mileage of 45 kilometers.
