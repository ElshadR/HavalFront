HAVAL's Success in the Ukrainian National Automotive Award of the Year “Auto Leader 2021”: HAVAL H2 Won in the Category "Leader of Price and Quality"
19.10.2020

“We are proud of the achievement of HAVAL H2 because this award is the result of our team efforts to create the right image of the model. - comments Boris Tkalenko, director of the distributor “New Energy of Ukraine”. After all, buyers choose H2 not because of its extraordinary design and not because of the latest technology. The main advantage of the model is balance. The HAVAL H2 New is an excellent solution for all those who value the "golden mean" and prefer a good price/quality ratio. " The success of HAVAL H2 if based on its high quality and right market positioning. When designing HAVAL models, the GWM follows the corporate principle of "Improve little by little every day", implementing the most modern technologies and innovations in new models. Compared to its predecessor, the HAVAL H2 New demonstrates a higher level in terms of design aesthetics, technology and safety, while the price of the new product always remains attractive, thanks also to special offers. At the beginning of 2020 the distributor launched an all-Ukrainian image advertising campaign "Your Formula of Comfort", which presented the HAVAL H2 New as an example of an optimal solution and a reasonable car choice. Through the two-dimensional illustration of the "formula of comfort" on advertising posters, the HAVAL H2 New actually embodies comfort in 4 manifestations: in comfort technologies (intelligence), in interior comfort (convenience), in price comfort (affordability) and in the comfort of making a decision to buy a car. (optimality). By integrating different “dimensions” of comfort, the HAVAL H2 is a model of affordable mobility, which means getting enough equipment at a reasonable price.

HAVAL brand has been dedicating to build a high quality , exquisite and professional product lineup, and win the consumers' trust by building a brand service of "moving the customers".
